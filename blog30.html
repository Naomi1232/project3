STRAWBERRY MUFFIN RECIPE

Indulge in the ultimate strawberry treat with our ridiculous easy Strawberry Muffin recipe! These babies are ridiculous moist,packed with a fresh strawberries,and can be whipped up in a minute - no fuss,no muss. Pair with a steaming cup of coffee or tea for the perfect me-time moment.

Why We Love This Recipe?

Strawberries and muffins are a match made in heaven -it's surprising they're not a staple! Our muffins boast a perfectly balanced texture: crispy on the outside and irresistibly moist within.

If muffins aren't your thing,don't worry! We've got you covered with our chewy almond cookies or homemade ginger kisses - pure bliss!

What are Strawberry Muffins?

Indulge in the sweetness of fresh strawberries perfectly balanced in a light and airy vanilla muffin.This effortless recipe comes togehter in minutes, and before you know it,your kitchen will be filled with the aroma of freshly baked goodness straight from the oven.

We picked up the secret to the irresistible strawberry muffins from our favorite chef,Roberta, in Bologna, Italy.Using her treasured, handwritten recipe, we quickly mixed and backed a batch of these sweet, sugar - dusted treats.

As we hovered by the oven door, mesmerized by the baking magic,Roberta caught us in the act.When I reached for the door handle,she playfully swatted my hand away, cautioning, "Don't dare open the oven door while desserts are baking! You'll let the magic escape, and they'll fall flat." Wise words from a seasoned baker!"

We're not alone in our fascination with the oven's magic, are we? Watching those humble batter blobs transform into fluffy, golden treats is a true delight!

Have patience and resist the urge to peek! The wait will be worth it - trust us, the end result will be pure perfection!

Roberta's parting wisdom?Bake with love, and you'll always get it wrong .

What You Will Need ?

Baker's dream come true! This recipe requires just a handful of simple ingredients:

  1. 1 egg
  2. 1/2 cup of milk
  3. 25 g Butter softened or melted (15 seconds in microwave)
  4. 1 cup fresh Strawberries
  5. 150 g flour
  6. 125 g sugar
  7. 8 g Baking powder
  8. 1/2 tsp Vanilla Essence

Got self-raising flour? No problem! You can skip the baking powder and use the same amount of self-rising flour as you would plain flour.

No self-raising flour and baking powder? No worries! Mix 1/2 tsp(teaspoon) baking powder with 1/2 tsp lemon juice or vinegar as a substitute

How To Make This Simple Strawberry Muffin Recipe:

Let's start by pre-heating the oven to 180 degree Celsius(360 degree Fahrenheit).(Plus, alittle pro-tip from Roberta - if your oven is open is capable,set the humidity to 10%)

Pop your flour,sugar and baking powder into a small mixing bowl.Give it a quick stir and leave a hole in the middle.

Next,crack the egg in the centre and add the vanilla essense before mix it through just a little.Pour in half te milk and mix again,then add the remaining milk and mix one more time.Finally add in the melted butter and combine, stirring throughly for a minute or two(or on low to medium speed if a using a mixer).

Now it's time to add the strawberries! Fold them through gently with a wooden spoon so they stay nice and chunky.

Carefully spoon your muffins mixture into patty pans, filling each 3/4 to the top for nice tall muffins.Pop them in the oven to bake for around 25 minutes or until gorgeously golden brown.

Finally a super simple Strawberry Muffin Recipe!They're like ME-TIME in a patty pan,just add a hot mugg of coffee or tea.
